ssVEP curves for patients with CRB1-related retinal dystrophy

Signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) at the 15 Hz flickering frequency of for the left (OS) and right eye (OD) across different spatial frequencies for each subject, ordered by visual acuity of the better eye. The mean signal of the control group (n = 16), along with the 75th percentile and the full range, is depicted in green and light green, respectively, for reference. The dotted grey area represents the noise floor where signal ≤ noise.

Correlation between ssVEP AUC and Visual Acuity

Correlation analysis between ssVEP Area under the curve (AUC) and visual acuity across all subjects and for CRB1 and Controls separately. Visual acuity was measured using ETDRS charts. Depicted are Pearson correlation coefficients and uncorrected p-values.

Correlation between ssVEP signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) and visual acuity at each spatial frequency (sf). Visual acuity was measured using ETDRS charts. Depicted are Pearson correlation coefficients and uncorrected p-values.
